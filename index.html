Publications

Deep learning for microplastic detection in aquatic environment

The main objective of this review paper is to examine and summarize current approaches for deep learning-based microplastic detection in aquatic environments. It offers a thorough summary of the methods currently used in the field rather than introducing new datasets or models. The article's objectives are to describe several detection techniques, point out their advantages and disadvantages, and talk about how deep learning is helping to advance microplastic detection technology. It is a tool for comprehending the current situation and directing the course of further research in this field.
